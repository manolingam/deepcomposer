About Us

The AWS DeepComposer Community is a group interested in machine learning, music and how to combine the two. In fact many of us are neither musicians nor machine learning experts, we are just people driven by curiosity and a creative mindset. Many of us spent last year learning through playing with AWS DeepRacer and are a part of the AWS DeepRacer Community. The two share the communication space and together form the AWS Machine Learning Community where we can both learn and share our experiences.
